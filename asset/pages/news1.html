WORLD SUPERSTAR THE WEEKND CONFIRMED FOR THE BRITS

THE BRIT AWARDS WITH MASTERCARD 2021

Tuesday May 11, 2021 - At Arena 02 on ITV and ITV Hub

The BRIT Awards 2021 with Mastercard today announce that global superstar The Weeknd, who had the world's biggest-selling single in 2020, will join this year's BRITs line-up, alongside Arlo Parks, Coldplay, Dua Lipa, Griff, Headie One, Olivia Rodrigo and Rag'n'Bone Man & P!nk with the Lewisham and Greenwich NHS Trust Choir.

The Weeknd's latest album After Hours - which spawned the record-breaking single "Blinding Lights" - went to #1 in over 26 countries upon its release last year, and it currently holds the record as the first male artist in history to achieve three #1 hits on the Billboard Hot 100 from the same album in three different years. The UK's biggest track of 2020, "Blinding Lights", has spent a total of eight weeks at the top of the Official Singles Chart. The singer-songwriter Canadian is nominated for the third time for International Male Solo Artist at this year's BRITs and last performed at the show in 2016. Due to current restrictions, the exclusive performance will be filmed remotely.

The BRIT Awards announced last month that it would be the first major indoor music event to welcome back a live audience – with 2,500 tickets donated by the music industry recorded to frontline heroes and their guests via a ballot (which is now closed). The indoor ceremony and live performance will be part of the scientific research program on government events, using improved testing approaches to examine how events can take place without the need for social distancing, meaning BRITs work closely working with government to meet safety guidelines. And as the first show of live music at the O2 arena for over a year, will play an important role in paving the way for the return of music live on a large scale as the UK emerges from last year's restrictions.

This year's BRIT Awards will take place on May 11 at the O2 arena, broadcast exclusively on ITV and ITV Hub and hosted by award-winning comedian and actor Jack Whitehall. The nominations were revealed at the end of March, with Celeste, Dua Lipa, Joel Corry and Young T & Bugsey joining Arlo Parks in receiving three nominations each, and Jessie Ware, J Hus, Bicep, AJ Tracey for two awards as with Headie One.

Griff has already been named this year's Rising Star Award winner.

Mastercard returns as Principal Partner for the 23rd year, having supported the BRITs for over two decades, bringing cardholders closer to music through a range of priceless experiences - from tickets to the BRITs, or Priceless Surprises with some of the UK's best-loved artists. This year, Mastercard will continue to sponsor for the first both the highly sought after Mastercard album award, as well as the UK single with Mastercard and is proud to continue connecting people have invaluable opportunities. Amazon Music is the official digital music partner for a second year and will host the official BRIT Awards 2021 playlist and will sponsor the male and female solo artist categories.
