FIRST WORLD STADIUM TOUR

ANNOUNCES STEP 1 OF AFTER HOURS TIL DAWN TOUR WITH SPECIAL GUEST DOJA CAT

TOUR TO PRESENT THE MUSICAL EXPERIENCE OF THE LATEST TWO ALBUMS FOR THE FIRST TIME LIVE

LAUNCHES THE XO HUMANITARIAN FUND WITH THE UNITED NATIONS WORLD FOOD PROGRAM. $1 FROM EACH TICKET TO BENEFIT THE FUND PLUS A COMBINED DONATION OF $1.5 MILLION.

The After Hours Til Dawn stadium tour will begin in the north of the hometown of The Weeknd, Toronto, Friday July 8 at the Rogers Centre, with stops at iconic American stadiums, including Metlife in New York, Soldier Field in Chicago, Hard Rock Stadium in Miami, Allegiant Stadium in Las Vegas, and more, before heading conclude on Friday, September 2 at SoFi Stadium in Los Angeles, California. The tour is produced by Live Nation. This is the first stop on the massive world tour by The Weeknd, with additional dates in Mexico, South America, Asia, Australia, New Zealand, Europe, Africa and the Middle East to be announced shortly.

Tickets for the North American dates go on sale starting Thursday, March 10 at 10 a.m. local time.

Fans who already had tickets for the After Hours arena tour in America of the North will have access to a special pre-sale from March 4 and will receive directly an email before then with more information.

Verizon will offer an exclusive pre-sale for The Weeknd's After Hours Til Dawn Tour to United States through the Verizon Up loyalty program. Members will first have access with the purchase of pre-sale tickets for certain shows starting Monday, March 7 at 10:00 a.m., local time, until Wednesday, March 9 at 10:00 p.m. local time. Verizon Up members will be invited to connect their Verizon accounts to their Ticketmaster accounts at ticketmaster.com to finalize their purchase. For more details and to sign up, visit Verizon Up.

There will also be a Spotify Fans First presale for top The Weeknd streamers starting March 8, as well as Live Nation presales in all markets.

The United Nations World Food Program (WFP), the largest organization humanitarian in the world fighting against world hunger, joined forces with its ambassador of goodwill Abel "The Weeknd" Tesfaye to launch the XO HUMANITARIAN FUND to which he contributes $1 on every ticket sold in North America for this highly anticipated tour. The Weeknd will also a donation of $500,000 and $1 million will be provided by the Food Program United States to the fund. Fueled by her passion for helping those in need, the multi-platinum and diamond certified global superstar created this fund to support aid essential food from the United Nations World Food Program to communities in on the verge of famine, in countries like Ethiopia, Madagascar and Yemen. The Fund is administered by the United States World Food Program.

This 2022 tour marks the first time The Weeknd will tour his mammoth album After Hours (her hit single "Blinding Lights" was named the No. 1 new song on the Billboard Hot 100 of all time, surpassing Chubby Checker's 1960 hit "The Twist") as well as the album recently released and highly critically acclaimed DAWN FM.

The tour will see its most ambitious production to date reflecting the creative journey that continues to unfold for these two albums, creating worlds within worlds as we have all seen happen unfold in various television performances, music videos and short films bringing these two first pieces of his trilogy to life.

The musical special immersive expands the multi-Grammy winning and critically acclaimed album Dawn FM, into a mesmerizing visual, creating a theatrical performance event that enveloped the audience in its annoying and sullen world.
