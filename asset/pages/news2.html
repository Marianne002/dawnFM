NEW VIDEO CLIP “TAKE MY BREATH” JUST RELEASED

THE FIRST SINGLE FROM THE ALBUM TO BE DISCOVERED DURING THE OLYMPIC PRIME TIME SHOW AS WELL AS ALBUM NEWS VIA GQ GLOBAL LAUNCH

NEW YORK, NY (August 6, 2021) – The Weeknd releases his highly anticipated single “Take My Breath”, the first of his upcoming album as announced in his GQ global cover story this week. The sonically-laden track showcases The Weeknd's ability to constantly evolve his sound and his talent for telling stories through his music.

The Weeknd teased new material with a series of tweets, culminating in a trailer austere message that he shared last Sunday. On Monday morning, GQ launched the first ever global issue in 90 years with The Weeknd as the only cover star of all editions. In the interview accompanying him with GQ, The Weeknd hinted at what listeners might anticipate with the next album. The same day, The Weeknd revealed a sample of "Take My Breath", as well as the release date, in a promotional teaser of the Olympics during NBC's telecast Olympics Prime Time Show featuring the Team USA women's track and field team.

"Take My Breath" marks The Weeknd's final track since the release of his album After Hours, critically acclaimed, released in March 2020. After Hours is The Weeknd's fourth No. 1 album in the United States and is certified double platinum by the RIAA with over 15.7 billion streams. In addition After Hours topped the charts in 20 other countries, including Canada and the United Kingdom.

“It will be the best project he has ever done. » - Mark Anthony Green on new music from The Weeknd in the September 2021 issue of GQ

“'It has become almost impossible to find anything else to listen to. Everything else sounded sweet. Or doesn't groove enough. Or felt too happy. Or too sad. It was clear to me that this is not not just the album The Weeknd always wanted to make; it’s the album we always wanted him to make. » - Mark Anthony Green in GQ
